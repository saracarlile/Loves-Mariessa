The Designer - Mariessa

Loves, Mariessa is an independent Fashion Designer from Austin, Texas. She was raised in Atlanta, GA and started designing clothes at the age of 15. Mariessa's mother is a seamstress, and with her mother's guidance, she started creating her own designs in high school. Mariessa then attended Bauder Collage in Atlanta and was schooled the technicalities of the fashion industry. After some time in college in Georgia, she moved to Austin and then traveled to the West Coast. She attended the local community college out West, Monterey Peninsula College, and took some patterns and alterations classes. Mariessa has produced fashion shows in Georgia, California, and many here in Austin, Texas.

Her brand is centered around creating classy attire for men and women, but she has done a great deal of costume/custom piece work
